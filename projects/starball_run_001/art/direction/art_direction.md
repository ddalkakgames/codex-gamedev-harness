# Art Direction

## Visual Identity

Starball Run uses a stylized celestial workshop theme: pale stone, deep blue metal, gold trim, cyan energy, and bright readable gameplay objects.

## Shape Language

| Category | Shape Direction | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Player | Small rounded star-traveler silhouette | Generic mannequin look |
| Star shards | Faceted gold crystal fragments | Perfect flat icons only |
| Energy orbs | Round cyan glass shell with glowing core | Same shape as star shards |
| Hazards | Dark compact devices with orange hot cores | Invisible or decorative-only danger |
| Goal gate | Tall readable arch with portal center | Small trigger-only marker |
| Platforms | Flat readable top surfaces with trim | Overly decorative unreadable surfaces |

## Color Roles

| Role | Color Intent |
|---|---|
| Safe / playable | Pale stone and stable blue metal |
| Reward | Gold and warm sparkle |
| Bonus reward | Cyan glass and energy glow |
| Danger | Orange/red heat and warning rings |
| Goal | Purple/white portal with gold frame |

## Readability Rules

- The gameplay path should be readable before background decoration.
- Reward, danger, and goal objects must differ by silhouette and color.
- Platform tops must remain flat and visually clear.
- VFX should reinforce gameplay state, not obscure collision.
