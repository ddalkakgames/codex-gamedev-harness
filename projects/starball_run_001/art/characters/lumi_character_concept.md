# Lumi Character Concept

## Role

Lumi is the player character for Starball Run: a small star-powered runner designed for fast readability in a 2.5D platformer camera.

## Visual Direction

| Topic | Direction |
|---|---|
| Silhouette | Small rounded body with a clear star motif |
| Proportion | Compact, toy-like, readable at distance |
| Color | Warm star core with blue/cyan accent energy |
| Face / expression | Simple, friendly, readable without heavy detail |
| Motion | Bouncy run, quick jump anticipation, bright landing feedback |

## Animation Intent

- Idle: gentle floating or breathing motion.
- Run: quick, light, energetic steps.
- Jump: clear squash/anticipation and upward burst.
- Land: small star sparkle and stable recovery.
- Collect: brief glow pulse toward the collected item.

## Modeling Notes

Lumi can start as a simplified proxy for v0.3. The first production target is a readable silhouette, not final facial or costume detail.
